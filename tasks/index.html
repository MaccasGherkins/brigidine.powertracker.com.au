Level 1 - 1 point for each task completed

1.1 Save Powering Change website into your favourites
1.2 Turn off Bluetooth and WIFI on your phone
1.3 Reduce screen brightness on your laptop
1.4 Ask your teacher for electronic copies instead of photocopied handouts
1.5 Switch hunters -Turn off fans, lights and computers at school

Level 2 - 20 points for each task completed

The tasks from level 2 onwards may require a "Leader of Change" [LOC] to validate your achievement. To do this you will need to post a photo onto your cluster page that shows you completing the task. You may need to take a screen shot of your computer and post this onto your cluster page, for example task 2.1

2.1. Become a Greenpeace member [View email / newsletter addressed to them]
2.2. Buy rechargeable batteries [Photo evidence]
2.3. Bring a reusable drink bottle to school [Honour point]
2.4. Become a Getup member [View email / newsletter addressed to them]
2.5. Switch hunters - Turn off standby power at home (on TVs anything that uses a Remote control) [Honour point]

3.4. Bring in an old phone to be recycled [photo evidence of phone being recycled]

4.1 Find fridge settings and turn the temperature up by +1c (Photographic evidence)
4.2 Put BCR Powering Change sticker on your car (Photographic evidence)
4.3 Attend a garage sale (Photographic evidence)
4.4 Become a One Million Women [1MW] member (view email / newsletter ]
4.5 Water the Edible garden in the Delay quad either before school recess lunch (honour point)

5.1 Order a vegetarian dish at a restaurant rather than a meat dish (Photo evidence,)
5.2 Take a photo of a house in your suburb that have solar panels installed on its roof (Photo evidence)
5.3 Switch to Power shop [or similar green energy broker] (View App installed on phone)
5.4 If your buddy has made over 200 points (name of student buddy)
5.5 Share to all friends a climate change article on social media (Photo evidence)
